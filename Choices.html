The Beginning of the end

You open your eyes with sense of unease. The dim morning light filters through the torn curtains of the apartment.

The sound of muted moans and scratching can be heard outside the door. You've been locked in this apartment for a few days now, terrified by what's happening outside.

It started as an ordinary day in the city. You were just a simple person, but just a few weeks ago, everything changed.

A deadly epidemic spread, turning people into flesh-hungry cratures, zombies.

Your building has become your last refuge.

You barricaded the entrance with everything you could find. Supplies are running low, and the situation is becoming unbearable.
